How Sustainable Is Our City?

Air

Poor air quality is a major health risk. Focus is shifting to cleaner transportation and vehicles.

Water

Groundwater depletion is a concern. Recharge and revival efforts are being promoted.

Transport

Unplanned growth is affecting mobility. Sustainable urban planning is needed.

Sanitation

Waste segregation and decentralized management are key for daily waste output.

Forest

Forest cover is declining due to encroachment. Protection is essential.

Energy

Electricity demand is rising. Power outages call for better infrastructure.

Women Safety

Ensuring safety for women in public and private spaces remains crucial.
